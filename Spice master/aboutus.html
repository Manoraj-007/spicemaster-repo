About Us

Spice Masters International

SMI is Indian Green Cardamom and Black Pepper exporter, manufacturer, producer & supplier from Bodinayakanur, Tamilnadu, India since 2016. SMI was set up as a Proprietorship entity during November 2017.

SMI is involved in trading of premium varieties of Cardamom procured from Planters of Idukki District, Kerala, and from Auction Houses conducted by the Spices Board India. Managed by well-experienced professionals, our team always focuses on implementing better quality standards and creating brand value in Domestic and overseas markets.

SMI engages around 25 women employees in the processing unit. Mr. Manikandan is keen on Women Empowerment and providing livelihood to the local community.

Sustainability

Organic Transitioning

We are committed to transitioning towards organic farming practices to maintain environmental health and reduce pollution levels.

Our organic transitioning program works closely with farmers to adopt sustainable agricultural methods that preserve soil health, protect biodiversity, and ensure the long-term viability of spice cultivation.

This initiative not only benefits the environment but also produces healthier, more flavorful spices for our customers worldwide.
