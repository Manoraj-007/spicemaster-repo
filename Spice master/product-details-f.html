Premium Green Cardamom Pods

Product Description

Finest quality whole green cardamom pods, known for their intense aromatic flavor and sweet, citrusy notes. Perfect for both sweet and savory dishes, and traditional Indian beverages.

Sourcing Information

Sourced directly from the high-altitude plantations of Idukki, Kerala, India, where traditional farming methods ensure optimal flavor and aroma.

Quality Specifications

Grade A, 8mm+ size, vibrant green color, high volatile oil content (min 6%), hand-sorted for purity, free from artificial colors and preservatives.
